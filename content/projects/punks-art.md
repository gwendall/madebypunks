Punks.art is a creative space exploring generative art and interactive tools inspired by the CryptoPunks aesthetic. A personal playground featuring experiments, visual studies, and developer tools.

## Projects & Tools

- **API** - Programmatic interface for creating punks and derivatives
- **PunkMaker** - Studio for remixing CryptoPunks with traits, colors, and presets
- **PUNK.CAM** - Augmented reality experience allowing users to become punks
- **Punks Wall** - Interactive infinite grid for exploring the entire collection
- **Diptychs** - Curated punk pairs examining rhythm and contrast
- **Nudes** - Full-body renderings with complete character designs
- **OnlyPunks** - Narrative scenes featuring accessories and pixel artwork
- **Rain** - Generative visual with matrix-style digital precipitation
- **Marketplace** - Market analysis tools with playful data visualization
- **Twins** - Discovery tool identifying punks with similar appearances

A laboratory for pixel art experiments built around the CryptoPunks aesthetic.
