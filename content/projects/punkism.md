Punkism reimagines CryptoPunks through various artistic movements and styles - constantly deconstructing and recontextualizing cultural icons. The series spans suprematism, brutalism, cubism, modernism, and contemporary pixel art aesthetics.

## Artistic Movements

- **Suprematism** - Geometric abstraction and minimalism
- **Pop Art** - Warhol-inspired tributes like Six Marilyns
- **Classical** - Rembrandt Punk and Dutch master homages
- **Cubism** - Fragmented, abstract approaches
- **Meme Culture** - Crying Wojak Punk and internet aesthetics

## Notable Works

- **Rembrandt Punk** - Classical portraiture meets pixel art
- **Six Marilyns** - Warhol-style pop repetition
- **Artist with Masks** - After James Ensor
- **Andy** and **Andy II** - After Duane Michals
- **Still Lifes** and **Vanitas** series
- **Glass Punk** - Transparent material studies

Featured in the Art of Punk exhibition alongside Beeple, Snowfro, and Tom Sachs. Part of PIV's broader practice that includes physical oil paintings and on-chain NFT collections.
